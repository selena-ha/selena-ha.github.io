Perfect Property

Find the home for you.

Role: UX Designer, UI Designer

Duration: 2 months

Tools: Figma, Slack

Background

Real estate investment is an increasingly popular way for individuals to achieve financial security. The abundance of blogs and agencies providing information may be overwhelming for buyers new to the market. This web app will provide them with the expertise needed to get started efficiently.

Problem

New buyers entering the real estate market may have a challenging time knowing where to start because there are so many things to take into account, including location, cost, and type of property. Additionally, the process of searching for properties can be time-consuming and complicated because there is a lot of data to sort through.

Objective

Create an app where users are able to search for properties using suitable search criteria entered by the user. When the user finds a property listing of choice, they are able to easily view it and return to it. To have a smoother experience, users will have access to relevant and comprehensive information about properties. It will be quick and easy to use it on the go.

How I got there

EMPATHIZE

Competitive Research

Before outlining the user journey and defining the path they would take, I wanted to gain insights into the thoughts and challenges users faced while using existing real estate apps. I delved into the reviews of two real estate apps available on the Google Play store. These apps featured functions such as a search and filter option, providing home information, and allowing users to save preferences through individual accounts.

A recurring issue highlighted in the reviews that impacted users' overall experience revolved around the search function. Users expressed difficulty in accessing the filter function to narrow their searches, resulting in a slower experience. Additionally, the filter lacked essential options, hindering users from finding homes that aligned with their specific goals.

The Solution

By taking note of the users' feedback in the reviews, I aim to develop a real estate app that will highlight the effectiveness of searching, emphasizing the utilization of filters and the map function. I will strategically outline the app's flow to better organize the information and construction of the app.

User Persona

To ensure the app will provide an intuitive but informative experience, I created a user persona that is new to the real estate market and would like to get to know more about the tools she can use to achieve her goals.

User Flow

To better understand and visualize the roadmap users will follow to achieve her goals, I built a user flow diagram. This visual representation helped me understand how users will interact with the platform and ensure a smooth and intuitive journey.

Conclusion
